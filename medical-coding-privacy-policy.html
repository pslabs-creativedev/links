Medical Coding

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Medical Coding mobile application collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app.

The app is designed for educational and reference use related to medical coding.

App URL: Google Play listing

Effective date: April 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We do not require account creation to use the app.

Based on the current app features, the app may store limited information on your device, such as:

  • Saved or favorited codes
  • Quiz or practice history and scores
  • Downloaded coding, lesson, and practice content
  • Basic in-app preferences needed to support app functionality

We do not intentionally collect personal health information or patient records through the app’s current core features.

2. How Information Is Used

The app uses stored information only to provide app features such as:

  • Searching and viewing medical coding content
  • Saving favorite codes for quick access
  • Tracking quiz performance and progress history
  • Downloading updated educational datasets and app content
  • Improving continuity of use on your device

3. Internet Access and Remote Content

The app uses internet access to check for and download updated datasets, lessons, and practice content.

These requests are used to deliver app content. The app is not designed to submit your quiz history, saved codes, or personal information to our servers as part of normal app use.

4. Data Storage

App content and study-related data are primarily stored locally on your device.

If you uninstall the app, locally stored information may be removed unless retained by your device backup settings.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your locally stored app data with third parties except where required by law, needed to protect legal rights, or necessary to operate essential infrastructure used to deliver downloadable app content.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on standard Android platform services and network infrastructure to download content and run properly.

If future versions of the app add analytics, ads, cloud sync, sign-in, or support tools, this Privacy Policy should be updated before those features are released.

7. Medical Coding Disclaimer

This app is for educational and reference use only. It is not official coding guidance, not medical advice, and not a substitute for the latest ICD-10-CM guidelines, payer rules, compliance review, or professional coding judgment.

8. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information stored by the app. However, no app, device, or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Your Choices

Local App Data

You can clear app data using your device settings or uninstall the app.

Saved Content

You can remove favorites and clear quiz history using app features where available.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date on this page.
