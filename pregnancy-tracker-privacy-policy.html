Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Pregnancy Tracker collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Effective date: April 14, 2026

About the App

Pregnancy Tracker is a pregnancy support app that helps users follow weekly updates, track baby movements, save growth entries, and keep personal notes.

Google Play listing: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.sp.pregnancytracker

Information We Collect

The app may store information that you enter directly, such as:

  • Your name, baby name, date of birth, due date, and last menstrual period.
  • Kick counter sessions, notes, and growth tracking entries.
  • Settings and preferences such as measurement units and app options.

This information is primarily stored locally on your device so the app can work properly for you.

Advertising

Pregnancy Tracker uses Unity Ads to show advertisements. Advertising partners may collect certain device information, identifiers, usage information, or diagnostic information as part of delivering and measuring ads.

Please review Unity’s privacy information for more details: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Provide personalized pregnancy tracking features.
  • Save your sessions, notes, and app preferences.
  • Improve app stability, functionality, and user experience.
  • Display ads supported through Unity Ads.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by third-party services that support app functionality or advertising, such as Unity Ads, only as needed for those services.

Data Retention and Control

Data that you enter in the app remains on your device unless removed by you. You can clear your saved app data from within the app or by uninstalling the app from your device.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information used by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at creativedevx@gmail.com.
