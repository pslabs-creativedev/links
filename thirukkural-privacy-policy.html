Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Thirukkural ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Thirukkural mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not require account creation to use the app.

The app may store limited data locally on your device, such as:

  • Bookmarks
  • Recent reading history
  • Reading preferences such as font size
  • Notification and text-to-speech settings

This information is stored only on your device to improve your app experience.

Advertising

The app may display ads using third-party advertising services such as Unity Ads. These services may collect device-related or usage-related information as described in their own privacy policies.

We do not directly control the data collected by third-party ad providers.

Notifications

If enabled, the app may send daily local notifications with a Thirukkural verse. Notification preferences are stored on your device and can be changed anytime in app settings.

Text-to-Speech

The app may use your device's built-in text-to-speech services to read content aloud. This feature works using services available on your device.

How We Use Information

We use the limited locally stored information only to:

  • Save your bookmarks
  • Show recent reading history
  • Remember your app settings
  • Provide notifications if enabled
  • Improve overall app usability

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may rely on third-party services such as advertising providers, which may process data according to their own policies.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app data, but no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services, including advertising SDKs. Please review their privacy policies for more details.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
